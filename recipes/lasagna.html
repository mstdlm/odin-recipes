Worlds best lasagna

This lasagna recipe takes a little work, but it is so satisfying and filling that it's worth it!

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion an garlic
  • Tomato products
  • Sugar
  • Spices and seasoning
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheeses
  • Egg

Steps

  • Make the meat sacue
  • Cook the noodles
  • Make the ricotta mixture
  • Layer the lasagna
  • Cover with foil and bake
  • Let the lasagna rest before serving
